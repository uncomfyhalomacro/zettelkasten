#+title: Climate Change

Climate change is a _natural process_. However, human activities such as burning of _fossil fuels_ and _deforestation_ accelerated this natural phenomenon called [[id:72c49662-3a01-47a4-8d28-86058e743c73][Global Warming]] to /increasing global temperatures/,
and /sea level rise/. These also affects the livelihood for both the environment and human livelihood such as:
- increase damage to food crops
- famine
- poverty
- extinction of many species
- threats to [[id:518f0cc2-c05b-4e6e-97d2-ee56038f0168][biodiversity]].
