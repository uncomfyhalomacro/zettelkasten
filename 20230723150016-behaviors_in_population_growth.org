#+title: Behaviors in Population Growth

A [[id:3cf0bba6-e4d7-49ff-9609-afc48c6889b5][Population's Density]] can affect how rapidly it can grow or decline such as biotic factors like disease.

Some population control factors are not affected by population density e.g. abiotic factors like weather.
